Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set an alarm?
A: Go to the Alarm page, select time, choose a sound, and press Activate.

Q: Can I use multiple timers?
A: Only one timer works at a time; reset and start another if needed.

Q: How does the stopwatch work?
A: Go to Stopwatch page, press Start to count, Stop to pause, Reset to clear.
